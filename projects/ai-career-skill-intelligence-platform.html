What the platform aims to solve

Career choices are often made with incomplete or outdated information. This platform is designed to collect job data, identify recurring skill demands, rank skill relevance, and present useful predictions through an interface that makes trends easier to understand.

The idea is to help users move from "What should I learn next?" to a clearer, data-backed roadmap that reflects real hiring patterns and emerging skills.

Build Details

The system is being shaped as an end-to-end intelligence product, not just a standalone model demo.

01

Data collection

Scrapes job-related information and converts noisy listings into structured, analyzable records.

02

Skill ranking

Analyzes frequency, context, and relevance of skills to identify what matters most for target roles.

03

Prediction layer

Uses ML logic to highlight probable direction, demand changes, and useful next-skill suggestions.

04

Dashboard experience

Presents insights through a React-based interface so users can explore trends instead of reading raw tables.
