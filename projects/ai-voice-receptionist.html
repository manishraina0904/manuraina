What the project does

The AI Voice Receptionist is designed to automate spoken interactions that usually happen at the first point of contact. It listens to a user question, converts speech to text, interprets the intent, and replies using generated speech.

This creates a hands-free interaction model that can be useful for assistance, query routing, or simple automated support scenarios where a conversational interface is more natural than a form or text box.
